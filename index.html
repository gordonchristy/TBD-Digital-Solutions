Our Process

KEEP Framework™

Designing learning. Building engagement. Driving results. Every solution we create follows the KEEP Framework, whether we are building a course, website, campaign, training program, or consulting plan.

KEEP guides everything we do.

The framework starts with people, turns attention into action, and keeps improving through feedback, analytics, reflection, and performance.

K

Know Your Audience

We begin by understanding your learners, customers, employees, or community before we design anything.

  • Who are they?
  • What motivates them?
  • What problems are they trying to solve?
  • What do they already know?
E

Engage with Purpose

We design experiences that capture attention, communicate clearly, and reduce cognitive overload.

  • How do we make this memorable?
  • How do we encourage interaction?
  • How do we keep the message clear?
  • How do we meet people where they are?
E

Enable Action

Engagement is only the beginning. We create content that helps people learn, decide, purchase, or perform.

  • Can learners perform the skill?
  • Can customers find the right service?
  • Can employees apply the process?
  • Did the audience gain something useful?
P

Promote Growth

We help sustain success through feedback, reflection, analytics, performance, and continuous improvement.

  • What results are we seeing?
  • What needs to improve?
  • How do we deepen the relationship?
  • How does the solution keep growing?
